Hi! My name is Simar Vanvari, and I’m a rising senior in high school and the Director of the Management Team at Hearts for Healing. In this role, I help manage team operations, create presentation materials, and keep things organized so our outreach efforts run smoothly.

I joined Hearts for Healing because I’m passionate about mental health and making a real difference in people’s lives. I also started my school’s Mental Health and Mindfulness Club to give students a space to feel heard, supported, and connected. I especially enjoy working with kids and contributing to causes that bring comfort and positivity to others.

Outside of school and volunteering, I love spending time with my friends and staying involved in activities that bring people together.
