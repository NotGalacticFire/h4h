Ayesha Jamal

Saudi Arabia Chapter Vice President

Ayesha Jamal is a dynamic and results-driven leader with a strong track record in event management and student engagement. She serves as Head of Events for the Specialty Interest Group, where she led its growth from 6 to over 20 medical specialties.

She successfully coordinated 30+ workshops and hands-on sessions, both online and in-person, focusing on clinical skills, specialty insights, and research development. Through strategic planning and collaboration with faculty, Ayesha ensured each event delivered strong academic and practical value for medical students and professionals alike.

She also serves as the President of the Multicultural Club, representing over 900 students from 30 diverse cultural backgrounds, Ayesha successfully organized seven large-scale events, each attracting over 200 attendees. One of these signature events received national attention, earning a feature in the Saudi Gazette for its cultural and medical significance.
