Ainsley Racine

Atlanta, Georgia Chapter Outreach Coordinator

Hi, my name is Ainsley! I am so excited to be a part of Hearts for Healing Atlanta. I have always had an interest in healthcare, especially serving less privileged areas that do not have as much healthcare awareness.

Growing up, I saw the lack of healthcare education and proper hygiene experienced by my family in Guatemala. This has motivated me to serve those in need and pay forward all the privileges I have experienced that not all have access to.
