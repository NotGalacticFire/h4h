Makayla Anderson

Chatfield, Minnesota Chapter President

Hi! I’m Makayla Anderson, a passionate advocate for mental health, a CNA, and someone who has seen firsthand how emotional pain can be just as serious as physical wounds. I joined Hearts for Healing because I believe in the power of art, empathy, and conversation to support those struggling with their mental health.

As someone who has personally faced challenges and found healing through creative expression and community, I knew I wanted to bring this mission closer to home. That’s why I founded the Minnesota chapter—to create a safe space where youth can speak up, feel seen, and help break the stigma around mental health in our own communities.
