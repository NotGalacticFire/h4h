Ishani Malik

Basking Ridge, New Jersey Chapter Vice President

Ishani Malik brings a thoughtful balance of organization and empathy to Hearts for Healing. As Vice President of the Basking Ridge Chapter, she helps coordinate events, mentors fellow members, and keeps the chapter focused on meaningful service for patients and families.

With a heart for advocacy and a passion for building supportive communities, Ishani is committed to expanding the reach of Hearts for Healing throughout New Jersey.
