Bethuel Wekesa

Kenya Chapter Head of Events

Bethuel Wekesa is a medical student at Uzima University with a strong commitment to advancing healthcare access and education in underserved communities. He currently serves as the Local Officer in charge of the Standing Committee on Medical Education (SCOME) at the Uzima chapter, advocating for improved medical education and professional development.

In addition, Bethuel is the Treasurer of the Rotaract Club of Uzima University, an organization whose mission and vision align closely with community service and humanitarian efforts. Through these roles, he actively contributes to initiatives that support hospitals, promote sanitation, and deliver medical aid to those in need, in line with the mission of Hearts for Healing.
