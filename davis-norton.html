Davis Norton

Secretary

Hello, I'm Davis Norton, a rising senior at Ridge High School and the secretary for Hearts for Healing. I joined Hearts for Healing because I wanted the ability to better my community and those who help made and make it what it is.

Throughout my live I have always hoped everyone felt that they are appreciated no matter where they are in life, especially in places like veterans hospitals. This organization is how I believe that I can best best solve that problem while leaving an impact now and in the future in my current community and beyond.

Besides assisting with Hearts for Healing, I also enjoy hiking, skiing, traveling, photography, and aviation.
