Aditi Chaudary

Fremont, California Chapter Treasurer

Mental health is a crucial part of everyone’s life, so I participate in programs where we organize self-care events like making bracelets for a good cause. I’ve completed over 100 volunteer hours at my local library and am part of an Academy of Sciences, combining my love for problem-solving and medicine with engineering. Taking a college-level speech course and earning a 97% helped me build confidence in public speaking and communication—skills I hope to use to uplift our community.
