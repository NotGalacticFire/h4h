Husna Irfan Thalib

Saudi Arabia Chapter President

Husna Irfan Thalib is a medical student at Batterjee Medical College, consistently maintaining top academic performance. With over 300 volunteer hours across more than 50 activities, Husna has actively contributed to vitals screening, food box distribution, Hajj medical camps, orphanage outreach, support for cancer patients, and multiple beach and park cleanups.

She serves as Head of Research for Medical specialty program, mentoring over 40 research groups from concept to publication. Additionally, Husna manages BioMediZone for Cameroon and serves as the Research Leader for the Medicine4Youth Enrichment Program, providing free training and guidance to aspiring student researchers worldwide.

Husna is also President of the Influencers Club, where she drives creative engagement and communication initiatives, and serves as Student Representative at the Program Council, regularly meeting with the Program committee, to represent student feedback. She holds the position of Vice President for the Forsan Club, further demonstrating her leadership across mentoring students to achieve scholarships.

Her dedication and excellence have earned her multiple prestigious research awards at both local and international conferences. Husna's diverse leadership roles at more than 10 student organizations, commitment to research and community service reflect her passion for creating positive impact both within and beyond the medical college.
