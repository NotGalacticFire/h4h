By the Numbers

In just a few years, Hearts of Healing has reached numerous milestones and touched countless lives across the country. Here's an overview of what we've accomplished so far:

  • 300+ handmade cards delivered to patients in hospitals and nursing homes
  • 120+ gift packages delivered to local hospitals and medical facilities
  • 5+ active chapters across the U.S., spreading joy from coast to coast
  • 50+ volunteers dedicated to spreading positivity
  • 200+ hospitals, clinics, and care centers that have received our gifts

Impact Through Community Engagement

Our volunteers and supporters play a vital role in creating and delivering these small acts of kindness. Here's a look at how our community has been involved:

  • Volunteer Workshops: We host workshops where volunteers come together to make cards, create gift packages, and assemble care packages for patients. These workshops foster a sense of community and purpose.
  • Fundraising Events: Through local events such as bake sales, charity runs, and auctions, we raise funds to support our initiatives and bring more smiles to those in need.
  • Partnerships with Local Businesses: We collaborate with local businesses who donate materials and sponsor our events to ensure we can continue our work.
  • School and Corporate Involvement: Many schools and companies join us in spreading kindness by holding card-making events and contributing to our fundraising efforts.

Looking Ahead

We're just getting started. As we continue to expand our reach, we aim to:

  • Grow our network of volunteers and chapters nationwide.
  • Increase our hospital and clinic partnerships to reach even more patients.
  • Launch new community outreach programs to engage with more supporters and spread joy.
  • Offer more personalized experiences for patients, including special holiday cards and gifts.
