Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our mission

What is Hearts for Healing, and what does it do?

Hearts for Healing is a nonprofit that donates handmade gifts, cards, flowers, and letters to helping centers to bring healing and positivity to ones in need. Our team is passionate about giving back through creative, heartfelt support.

Who does Hearts for Healing support?

We focus on supporting medical centers, pantries, children's hospitals, Cancer centers, Veteran hospitals, Etc. Our mission is to uplift those in challenging situations through community-driven acts of kindness and creativity.

How can I donate items or money to the cause?

You can support us by contributing money to fundraisers (e.g., bake sales, GoFundMe, bracelet sales, etc.) or donating handmade items such as cards and care packages. Details for donations and events will be posted on our social media and website.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Donations are not tax deductible!

How can students or community members get involved?

Members can create new chapters across the United States, join our team, volunteer, or donate items to our fundraisers. Contact us via heartsforhealing2025@gmail.com or fill out the application form listed in the website.

What kinds of items can I donate?

We currently accept cards, hygiene products, letters, small decorations, flowers (real or artificial), and non-perishable care items.

Can I start a Hearts for Healing club at my school?

Yes! We welcome student leaders to form school-based chapters. Email us at heartsforhealing2025@gmail.com with your interest, and we'll help you get started with resources and steps.

Where does the money or donated material go?

All funds raised go toward purchasing materials for gifts, packaging, and delivering care packages to centers. Expenses are tracked by our Finance Lead, Ritvik Peddi. Transparency and purpose-driven budgeting are our top priorities.

How do you choose who to support?

We reach out to local helping centers and other facilities to understand their needs and interest in collaborating. Our Outreach Team, led by Rishi Santhanam and supported by members, tracks these connections carefully.

Who runs Hearts for Healing, and how can I contact the team?

Hearts for Healing is led by Co-Presidents Mayank Jadhav and Hasini Namburi, along with a dedicated team. For general inquiries or to get involved, email us at heartsforhealing2025@gmail.com or visit our website

Still have questions?

Contact us directly and we'll be happy to help!
